Classic Waffles

Description

The greatest item on any breakfast menu known to man. The classic waffle always delivers and is perfect for breakfast or brunch.

This recipe only takes 10 minutes of prep time and fifteen minutes of cook time. That's another great thing about waffles, they are easy to make and don't take long to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon sea salt
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of almond milk
  • 1/3 cup of butter melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  3. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
